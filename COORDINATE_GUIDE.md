# How to Find and Enter Proper Dealer Coordinates

## Quick Ways to Find Coordinates:

### Method 1: Quick-Select Cities (Easiest ⭐)
1. Go to Dashboard → Dealers
2. In the "Location" section, click "Quick Select City"
3. Choose your city from the dropdown list
4. Coordinates auto-fill! ✅

### Method 2: Use the Map Search (Easy)
1. Click on the location form
2. Look for 🔍 **Search box at the top-left of the map**
3. Type: Address, city name, or landmark
4. Click on the result
5. Map will auto-zoom and set your coordinates ✅

### Method 3: Click on the Map (Visual)
1. Zoom and pan to find your dealer location
2. Click exactly where the dealer is located
3. A marker will appear and coordinates update automatically ✅

### Method 4: Google Maps (Precise)
1. Open Google Maps in another tab
2. Search for your dealer's address
3. Right-click on the location → "What's here?"
4. You'll see coordinates like: `20.5937, 78.9629`
5. Copy the **latitude** (first number) → Paste in "Latitude" field
6. Copy the **longitude** (second number) → Paste in "Longitude" field
7. Click elsewhere to update the map ✅

### Method 5: Online Coordinate Tools
- https://www.gps-coordinates.net/ - Paste address, get coordinates
- https://maps.google.com/ - Right-click on location to get coordinates

## Understanding Coordinates:

- **Latitude**: North/South position (-90 to +90°)
  - Positive = North of Equator
  - Negative = South of Equator
  
- **Longitude**: East/West position (-180 to +180°)
  - Positive = East of Prime Meridian
  - Negative = West of Prime Meridian

## Tips:

✅ **Valid Examples**: `28.7041, 77.1025` or `40.7128, -74.0060`
❌ **Invalid**: `0, 0` or `0, 20` (won't appear on map)
✅ Coordinates can have up to 4 decimal places for accuracy
✅ Once you add a dealer, it will appear with a marker on the Global Dealer Locator map

## Verification:

After adding a dealer, go to the **Global Dealer Locator** page and:
1. Select the country
2. You should see the dealer's marker on the map
3. Click "📍 Locate on Map" to zoom to that location
